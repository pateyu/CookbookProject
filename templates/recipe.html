Delicious Chocolate Cake

Dessert
Gluten-Free Vegan Dairy-Free

This chocolate cake is a rich, dark, and moist delight that will satisfy anyone's sweet tooth, and it's vegan and gluten-free!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup gluten-free flour
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup coconut oil
  • 3/4 cup maple syrup

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix all dry ingredients in a large bowl.
  3. Add wet ingredients and mix until smooth.
  4. Pour into a baking dish and bake for 25 minutes.
  5. Let cool before serving. Enjoy your delicious, guilt-free dessert!
Prep Time: 15 min Cook Time: 25 min
